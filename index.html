My CCAs and projects

Interact Club

As someone who always looks for ways to serve the community and enjoys starting up projects to champion for a particular cause, I joined the Interact Club in JC and embarked on a casual racism project. It was aimed to raise awareness about casual racism since it mirrored my principles of social justice and equal treatment for all. Besides, we also provide for weekly outreach sessions to 4 different organisations namely the Cheshire Home,Institute of Mental Health, Marsiling Primary and TOUCH centre for independent living. Since my batch was affected by COVID and were unable to go down in person to volunteer, we held the outreach sessions via zoom and organised activities for them.
